How To upload a Project in projectHUB

  • 1. Inorder to upload a projects in projectHUB StudentLogin mandatory
  • 2. At the time of events/techfests if student done projects they can directly upload their projects through their respective ProjectHUB account.
  • 3. During Normal days students like to upload their projects in this portal . In this case respective department faculty will upload the projects.
    • steps to follow
      • i. StudentLogin is mandatory.
      • ii. In student Profile there will have a option "projectUpload" through this hyper link they should enter the details of the project.
      • iii. Projects details will be directed to the department Faculty member. Faculty will go through the details of the projects if she/he finds projects is good then she/he will upload requested project details into ProjectHUB portal.
      • iv. If Faculty deny the project . that project details can't available on ProjectHUB portal.
